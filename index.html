FAQ

Quick project notes.

Can applicants and HR managers both create accounts?

Yes. During signup, users choose Applicant or HR Manager and are routed to the correct dashboard.

Where does the data come from?

Only from signup, job, profile, and application forms saved to Supabase. The system does not preload fake records.

Can HR managers review applications?

Yes. HR managers can view submitted applications, change recruitment status, add notes, and schedule interviews.

Can this become a full HR system later?

Yes. Payroll, leave requests, employee records, onboarding tasks, and performance reviews can be added as new modules.
