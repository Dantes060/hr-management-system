Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to get started.

Who can create an account?

Applicants can register directly. HR accounts are assigned by an authorized administrator to prevent unauthorized access to candidate records.

How do candidates submit a CV?

Applicants can upload a PDF or Word document to private storage and optionally add a portfolio link.

Can HR managers review and update applications?

Yes. Authorized HR users can filter candidates, review full profiles, add notes, schedule interviews, and update recruitment stages.

Can the platform expand beyond recruitment?

Yes. Employee onboarding, leave, payroll, performance reviews, and document management can be added as separate modules.
